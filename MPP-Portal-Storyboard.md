# MPP Portal Interactive Walkthrough - Storyboard

## Overview

**Project**: MPP Portal eLearning Walkthrough
**Style**: Supademo-like guided tour with video narration
**Target Audience**: Mentors and Proteges in the DoD Mentor-Protege Program
**Goals**:
- Learn to navigate the MPP Portal
- Understand the program and its features

**Duration**: _TBD (depends on number of steps)_

---

## Step 0: Cover Screen (Welcome)

**Screen**: Full-screen cover with MPP logo centered
**Background**: Dark gradient or solid DoD blue
**Elements**:
- DoD MPP Logo (centered, prominent)
- Course title below logo
- Video narrator pops in (bottom-right or center)

**Video Narration** (Draft - ~20 seconds):
> "Welcome to the Mentor-Protege Program Portal. Whether you're a mentor looking to guide a small business or a protege ready to grow your capabilities, this walkthrough will show you how to navigate the portal, access key features, and get the most out of the program. Let's get started."

**After Video**:
- Continue button appears (glowing/pulsing)
- Clicking advances to Step 1

---

## Step 1: Learning Objectives

**Screen**: Animated learning objectives reveal
**Background**: Dark gradient matching cover
**Elements**:
- Heading: "What You'll Learn"
- 5 objectives animate in one-by-one (fade/slide)
- Avatar narrator in corner narrates each objective

**Learning Objectives** (Draft):

1. **Navigate the MPP Portal** — Find your way around the homepage, menu, and key sections
2. **Understand Mentor & Protege Roles** — Know what's expected of each party in the program
3. **Access Your Dashboard** — View applications, agreements, and program status
4. **Complete Your Company Profile** — Ensure your SAM registration and company info are up to date
5. **Submit and Track Applications** — Learn how to apply and monitor your progress

**Video Narration** (Draft - ~30 seconds):
> "By the end of this walkthrough, you'll be able to:
> Navigate the MPP Portal with confidence...
> Understand the roles of mentors and proteges...
> Access your personalized dashboard...
> Complete your company profile...
> And submit and track your applications.
> Let's begin by choosing where you'd like to start."

**Animation Style**:
- Each objective fades in as narrator reads it
- Checkmark or icon appears next to each
- Subtle glow/highlight on current objective

**After Video**:
- Continue button appears
- Clicking advances to Step 2 (Choice Screen)

---

## Step 2: Choose Your Path

**Screen**: Branching choice screen (video game style)
**Background**: Dark with subtle particle effects or grid
**Elements**:
- Heading: "Where would you like to start?"
- Two large buttons side-by-side (card style)
- Avatar in center or corner

**Button 1: "Explore the Portal"**
- Icon: Homepage/building icon
- Subtext: "New to MPP? Start here to learn the basics."
- Leads to: Portal Homepage walkthrough (Path A)

**Button 2: "Go to Dashboard"**
- Icon: Dashboard/chart icon
- Subtext: "Already registered? Jump to your dashboard."
- Leads to: Dashboard walkthrough (Path B - future)

**Video Narration** (Draft - ~10 seconds):
> "Where would you like to begin? If you're new to the program, start by exploring the portal. If you're already registered, head straight to your dashboard."

**Interaction**:
- Hover effect on buttons (glow, scale up)
- Click selects path
- Smooth transition to chosen walkthrough
